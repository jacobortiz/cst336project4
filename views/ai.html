Artificial intelligence (AI) is an area of computer science that emphasizes the creation
of intelligent machines that work and react like humans. Some of the activities computers
with artificial intelligence are designed for include: Speech recognition, learning, planning,
and problem solving
Machine learning is also a core part of AI. Learning without any kind of supervision requires
an ability to identify patterns in streams of inputs, whereas learning with adequate supervision
involves classification and numerical regressions.
